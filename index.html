Minesweeper

Rules

The board size is 16x16. It contains mines .

To win a round of Minesweeper 😎, you must click on the board every square that doesn't have a mine under it otherwise the game will be over 😵.

The number on a square shows the number of mines adjacent to it.

Put a flag in a zone when you have confirmed that there is a mine using the right mouse button. In case there is not a mine you will see at the end of the game.

Put a question mark ? when you suspect that there is a mine using the right mouse button.

Click the smiley face 🙂 if you want to reset the game.

Please remember that you 40 minuts ⏰. It starts the second you click on the board.

Good luck!
